Welcome to WN-Reporters!

The accredited team of editors who edit Wikinews — the free news source you can write!

Who are WN-Reporters?

WN-Reporters are accredited Wikinews volunteers trusted by the Wikinews community. Accredited reporters are certified for the purposes of carrying out investigative journalism, interviews and gathering information related to Wikinews articles.

However, the accredited reporters do not represent the Wikimedia Foundation.

What is the purpose of wn-reporters.org?

wn-reporters.org is an associated website run by Wikinews contributors as an adjunct to the news production process on Wikinews. wn-reporters.org empowers accredited reporters by providing them e-mail and related tools to help them publish original reports.

Have something you wish to be reported or want to be interviewed?

If you have some evidence for a news piece, or want to share some information, or want to request an interview? Please reach out to us sending an e-mail to scoop.

For WN-Reporters.

You are probably looking for webmail, passphrase reset tool, or the file storage. If you need any new tool or service, or if you notice something wrong with any of the services, please bring to mail-server admin's notice.

Want to help share the hosting costs?

wn-reorters.org is completely funded by Wikinews volunteers. If you would like to help share the hosting costs, you are very welcome. Please send your patronage via PayPal with the summary “Donation for wn-reporters”.
